# Cybersecurity Questions

## 1. What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, systems, and data from hackers, viruses, and cyber attacks.

---

## 2. What are the main types of cyber threats?

Some common cyber threats are:

- Malware
- Phishing
- Ransomware
- Hacking
- Viruses
- Data Breaches

---

## 3. What is the CIA Triad?

The CIA Triad means:

### Confidentiality
Keeping information private and secure.

### Integrity
Making sure data is correct and not changed.

### Availability
Making sure systems and data are available when needed.

---

## 4. What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking means legally testing systems and networks to find security weaknesses and improve protection.

---

## 5. How can cybersecurity help CoreTech Innovation?

Cybersecurity helps protect company data, customer information, and systems from cyber attacks. It also improves trust, privacy, and business security.
